Natalie’s Driving School is committed to developing life-long safe, confident and independent drivers.

Natalie’s Driving School is a newly established driving school, based in Goonellabah, that is owned and operated by Natalie Forster. Natalie is an accredited Driver and Rider instructor with a Bachelor of Education. She has been instructing learners and testing learner riders for 10 years and teaching students for nearly 20 years.

Driving lessons are conducted in a professional and safe environment. Driving lessons are offered in either a manual or automatic vehicle with dual controls that ensures students’ safety. Driving lessons are conducted across the Northern Rivers including Lismore, Ballina and Casino. All other areas are available for bookings upon request.
