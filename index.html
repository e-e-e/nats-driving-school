Jervis Bay Driving School is committed to servicing the youth of the Bay and Basin area of the Shoalhaven

Driving lessons can be conducted in the surrounding areas of Jervis Bay from Wandandian to Nowra. All suburbs are available for bookings upon request.

Driving lessons are conducted in a professional and safe environment. Students will be able to access sessions at times suitable to their (and their parents) lifestyles. It is the focus of this business that we offer convenience to the students, we will pick learner drivers up at home or from a place they specify and drop them off accordingly.

This will allow the students to access lessons prior to school, for example, be picked up from home, have a supervised driving lesson and be dropped at the school ready for their day. After school driving lessons can also be arranged for pick up from the school and finish at home, or at work for example. This offers both the parents and the students ease of access to driving lessons, ensuring that all Jervis Bay & surrounds local youth have an opportunity to secure driving lessons from a professional instructor.
